Universal Fucking Gift Set

International Society of Typographic Designers, ISTD, is a professional body run by and for typographers, graphic designers and educators. The Society has an international membership who share and support its aim to create and inspire interest in all forms of typographic communication.

Challenge

This brief was to research and showcase the origin of an interesting word that is commonly used today and how it came about being introduced into the english language, as well as displaying the infomation in a designed typographic piece.

Solution

I decided to use the word fuck as my interesting everyday word, as it has become one of the most universally used words in the dictionary. The word has multiple usages, ranging from pleasure to pain and everything in-between.
I created a universal gifting set that includes a wrapping paper, gift card and evelope. This is a "one-size-fits-all" solution to all your fucking gifting needs.
